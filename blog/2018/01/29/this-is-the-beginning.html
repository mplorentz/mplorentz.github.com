This Is The Beginning Of The Blog

Jan 29, 2018

This is the beginning of my blog. It is made with Jekyll. It is not federated or self-hosted, but I would like it to be. It might be more of a traditional blog, or more of the micro type. I’m not sure yet.

I want to use it as a journal of personal projects and a place to remember things that I think are cool. I would like to interact with my friends and family through it, but I’m not sure how I want that to happen just yet. Instead of waiting until I’ve figured out exactly what I will do with my blog, I shipped it. It will never be perfect, and if I wait until I know it will be it will probably never get made.
